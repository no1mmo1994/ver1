1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Cookies help websites function, remember preferences, and provide analytics and advertising insights.

2. Technologies We Use

We may use cookies, web beacons, tags, pixels, and local storage. This can include technologies from analytics and advertising partners.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Support core website operation and security.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: Help us understand website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Advertising and measurement cookies: Help evaluate ad campaign effectiveness, including event attribution.
  • Functional cookies: Remember settings and improve user experience.

4. Meta Pixel and Similar Tools

We use Meta Pixel and related event tracking to measure interactions such as page views, contact clicks, and lead submissions. This helps improve campaign relevance and performance reporting.

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies through browser settings and, where available, platform privacy controls. Blocking certain cookies may affect website functionality and measurement quality.

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer Do Not Track signals. Because there is no consistent industry standard, this website may not respond uniformly to those signals.

7. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services integrated with this website may place cookies subject to their own privacy and cookie policies.
